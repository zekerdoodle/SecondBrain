# Geometry Dash "Grief" - Technical Analysis
*Research compiled: January 2026*

## Overview
**Grief** is an upcoming extreme demon level expected to become the new top 1 on the Geometry Dash Demonlist. It is a remake of the 2015 version of Slaughterhouse.

**Creators:** Icedcave, DrCuber, Doop (collaborative effort)
**Verifier:** Doggie (DoggieDasher)
**Expected Placement:** Top 1 (above current #1 Thinking Space II)

---

## 1. Builder/Verifier Information

### Doggie's Background
- Previously verified **Slaughterhouse** (former top 1) in December 2021
- Recently verified **Deimos** (top 10 extreme demon)
- Highly experienced with extremely difficult levels

### Verification Progress (as of early 2026)
- **80%+ completion** reported
- **99% progress** reached (per TikTok content)

## 2. Technical Difficulty Breakdown

### Frame Perfect Requirements

**At 60 FPS:**
- **237 frame perfects total**
- Some sections marked in RED as **IMPOSSIBLE at 60 FPS**
- This means certain parts REQUIRE higher refresh rates to complete

**At 120 FPS:**
- **11 frame perfects**

**Comparison to Current Top 1 (Thinking Space II):**
- Thinking Space II: 108 total frame perfects
- **Grief has more than 2x the frame perfects** of Thinking Space II
- Thinking Space II has one 240fps frame perfect
- **Grief's 237 frame perfects at 60fps makes it the highest count among known levels**

### Section-by-Section Breakdown
Based on available data:

| Section | Frame Perfects (60 FPS) | Frame Perfects (120 FPS) | Notes |
|---------|-------------------------|--------------------------|-------|
| 0-10% (Beginning) | 30 | - | Heavy technical start |
| 26-35% (First Drop) | 23 | - | Dense clicking patterns |
| 35-44% (Wave Spam) | Nearly all clicks | - | Almost every input is frame perfect |
| 56-65% | 11 | 2 | Mixed precision requirements |
| Later sections | 19 | 1 | Sustained difficulty |

### FPS Requirements
- **Minimum viable FPS:** 120+ (some sections impossible at 60 FPS)
- **Recommended FPS:** 240-360 FPS for optimal playability
- **Competitive limit:** 360 FPS (Demonlist standard for pre-2.2 versions)
- **Post-2.2 update:** Any framerate permitted, but Demonlist still rejects records above 360Hz

### What Makes It So Hard
1. **Sheer volume:** 237 frame perfects means constant precision
2. **Wave spam section** (35-44%): Nearly every input is frame perfect
3. **FPS-locked sections:** Some parts literally cannot be done at lower refresh rates
4. **Complex click patterns:** Requires precise timing AND quick reflexes
5. **Tight corridors:** Minimal margin for error in positioning
6. **Abrupt speed changes:** Player must adapt instantly to different speeds
7. **Length + consistency:** Must maintain perfection throughout entire level

## 3. Community Discussion - Humanly Possible?

### The Debate
- Some community members question whether Grief can beat **"The Human Limit"**
- Positioned alongside other theoretical top extremes like **Silent Acropolis** and **Silent Bloodbath**
- Described as a "true test of skill and endurance"
- Verification status is crucial to determining actual possibility

### Factors Supporting Possibility
- Doggie has reached 99% (verification nearly complete)
- Previous "impossible" levels have been verified (e.g., Slaughterhouse)
- Modern players have access to 240-360Hz monitors
- FPS bypass technology allows optimal performance

### Factors Questioning Possibility
- 237 frame perfects is unprecedented
- Wave spam section with near-total frame perfect density
- Some sections physically impossible at 60 FPS
- Over a year of verification attempts by top player
- ~300,000 attempts estimated (extremely high)

### Current Consensus
- **Likely humanly possible** given Doggie's 99% progress
- **Will require verification to confirm** final placement
- Community awaiting official completion to assess true difficulty

## 4. Comparison to Current Top Levels

### Grief vs. Thinking Space II (Current #1)

| Metric | Thinking Space II | Grief |
|--------|-------------------|-------|
| **Current Rank** | #1 (verified Sept 2025) | Unverified (expected #1) |
| **Total Frame Perfects** | 108 | 237 (60fps) + 11 (120fps) |
| **120fps Frame Perfects** | 40+ | 11 |
| **240fps Frame Perfects** | 1 | Unknown |
| **Length** | 1:07 | Unknown |
| **Verification Attempts** | 83,913 (by Zoink) | ~300,000 (by Doggie, ongoing) |
| **Impossible Sections** | 9 physically impossible sections | Multiple sections impossible at 60fps |
| **Key Difficulty Factor** | Raw precision, input density | Frame perfect density, wave spam |

### Why Grief Is Expected to Surpass Thinking Space II
1. **More than double the frame perfects** (237 vs 108 at 60fps)
2. **Wave spam section** with near-total frame perfect density (unprecedented)
3. **Higher verification attempt count** (~300k vs ~84k)
4. **Longer verification time** (over a year vs. Thinking Space II's timeline)
5. **Multiple FPS-impossible sections** requiring high refresh rate monitors

### Demonlist Impact
When Grief, Heliopolis, and Orbit are verified, the top 10 will change for the first time in a long time, indicating these levels represent a new era of difficulty in Geometry Dash.
